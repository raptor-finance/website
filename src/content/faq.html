How do I buy Raptor Token?

Raptor is launched on the Binance Smart Chain. This means that in order to buy Raptor, you need the Binance Coin (BNB). You can buy BNB from Trustwallet, Binance Smart Chain wallet or an exchange like Binance itself, Crypto.com, Whitebit or similar. Once you have BNB, send them to your wallet and head over to PancakeSwap to buy Raptor for BNB. Make sure you set your slippage at 8%.

How to stake?

Go to the our "Staking" page. It will automatically ask you to connect your wallet, connect the wallet which contains your Raptor Tokens. You will see your wallet information appearing and the total amount of Raptor Tokens you own in your wallet. We do not charge any fees for staking but you have to have BNB in your wallet to pay for the Binance SmartChain gas fees. Make sure you keep enough BNB in your wallet to claim or unstake you Raptor Tokens in the future. When you are all set use the slidebar to select how many Raptor Tokens you wish to stake. Press the Stake button to stake the amount of Raptor tokens you entered. Now your Raptor Tokens are staked and you earn a 5% APY.

How to claim my rewards?

When you have staked your Raptor Tokens you can claim your rewards anytime you wish. Just press the claim button in our "Staking" page and your rewards are automatically transferred to your wallet. Make sure you have enough BNB in your wallet to pay for the gas fees.

How to unstake my Raptor Tokens?

Go to our "Staking" page and press the unstake & claim button. All your staked Raptor Tokens and your rewards will be automatically transferred to your wallet. Make sure you have enough BNB in your wallet to pay for the gas fees.

Why is the price in my Trustwallet sometimes incorrect?

Trustwallet derives its pricing from CoinMarketCap embedded API and this service has been known to have incorrect and inflated pricing on smaller tokens. One reason for this is that they don't update the price as frequently to save resources.
