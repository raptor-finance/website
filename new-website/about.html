About

We are Raptor finance a decentralized, financial ecosystem designed by holders for holders. Our mission is to heal planet earth and stop climate change by allowing our holders to stake their tokens to generate yield for themselves and ecological projects.

Here at Raptor Finance our goal is to enable the everyday person with an ability to earn compounding interest through purchasing our native token and staking it to our platform.

We are also implementing a very state of the art farming system, crypto lottery, and eventually one of the most adaptive NFT marketplaces.

Currently in development is our RaptorSwap exchange platform that will easily compete with PancakeSwap, UniSwap, SushiSwap, and any other token swap exchange due to the quality of the interface and our one of a kind 7% burn fee for transactions (applied to buys/sells) which has been designed to combat the exchange killer known as impermenant loss.

To mitigate the effects of impermenant loss, which is what other platforms suffer from, we know that it's not farming but for a majority of people to hold their tokens and stake them to the platform. Therefore we have greatly incentivized staking with no time-locks and a generous 5% APY.

No time locks ensures your ability to set up your own claiming and reinvesting schedule effectively giving you the ability to earn compounding interest from your investment in our native token.

In resolution we are the future of decentralized finance and liquidity staking, with a plan to change the world by donating part of our operating fees to community driven charities.
